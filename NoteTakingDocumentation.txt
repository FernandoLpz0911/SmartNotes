- BERT is a Natural Language Processing Model
    - Natural Language Processing Models
        - Field that studies interaction between computers and human language with the goal to enable machines to understand interpret
          and generate human language in a manner that is beneficial.
        - NLP combines computational linguistics with statistical models, machine learning, and deep learning. Goal to allow computers
          to process and analyze large amounts of text and speech data
        - In other words, NLP gives the tools and techniques on how to translate the raw data into something understandable for a computer
        - Significant because:
            - it allows interaction with computers using human natural language as shown with chatbots and virtual assistants
              but also with voice assistants.
            - Reads unstructured data and can manage it such that:
                - It helps determine tonality in text
                - It extracts key information from large documentation
                - Allows for summarizing of larger documentation
                - Can classify documentation utilizing the summarizing and extraction abilities
            - Helps with language translation
            - Improves information retrieval and search
            - Helps with automation and streamlining of tasks
        - KEY NLP Techniques:
            - Tokenization: Breaking down text into individual words, phrases, or tokens/symbols.
            - Stemming and lemmatization: Reduces the word into root form to check validity.
            - Stop word removal to get rid of common words that don't matter for analysis.
            - Part of Speech Tagging which identifies the grammar role of the words
            - Named Entity Recognition which identifies and classifies "named entities" like people or dates.
            - Sentiment Analysis: Determines emotional tone of a text.
            - Topic modeling: Discovering topics that come up in documentation.
            - Word Embeddings for semantic relationships between words. Fancy word for showing relationship between words.
            - Parsing which analyzes the grammatical structure of a sentence for word relation.
